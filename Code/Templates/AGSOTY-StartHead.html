This page displays Age Group Standings for Pacific Masters swimmers. These standings are determined by awarding swimmers points for:
  • Age group rankings within Pacific Masters in each pool course. (See SCY Rankings, SCM Rankings, LCM Rankings)
    • NOTE: AGSOTY pool discipline points are limited to best 8 swims in a course.
  • Placements in Pacific Masters open water events. (See this year's Open Water standings)
    • NOTE: AGSOTY open water points are taken from the category 1 accumulated points for the season.
  • Top ten in national rankings (See USMS SCY Rankings, USMS SCM Rankings, USMS LCM Rankings)
  • Pacific Masters records and USMS records set.

The detailed rules can be found here, and a nice description of how the Age Group Standings work can be found here.

This page will be updated periodically. Date of the last update is at the top of the page.

  • Rankings within Pacific Masters will be updated with all results that have been added to the USMS database.
  • Points for placements in Pacific Masters open water events will be updated roughly when the Open Water Points page is updated.
  • Points for the {YearBeingProcessed} season USMS Top Ten rankings will appear:
    • Short Course Yards: July or August, {YearBeingProcessed}
      • SCY season: June 1, {YearBeingProcessedMinusOne} through May 31, {YearBeingProcessed}
    • Long Course Meters: November or December, {YearBeingProcessed}
      • LCM season: {LCMStartOfSeasonDay}, {YearBeingProcessedMinusOne} through {LCMEndOfSeasonDay}, {YearBeingProcessed}
    • Short Course Meters: early March, {YearBeingProcessedPlusOne}
      • SCM season: January 1, {YearBeingProcessed} through December 31, {YearBeingProcessed}
  • USMS record points will be updated with all record swims appearing on the USMS National Records pages.

A swimmer will earn points in two different age groups if he or she ages up during the covered seasons. Beginning in 2018, if a swimmer ages up during the covered seasons, we will merge in points from the prior age group. See rules page for details.

To be eligible for our end-of-year age group awards, you must have participated in three Pacific Masters sanctioned competitions during the seasons covered by these standings.

Points are fluid and will change throughout the season as more swimmers compete in an event. They may also change as errors are uncovered.
